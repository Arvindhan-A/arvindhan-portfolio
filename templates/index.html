About

The short version

I'm a Class 12 student at Sandipani Vidyalaya in Chennai. I spend most of my time between condensed matter physics and writing Python scripts that talk to sensors.

I don't just read about how things work — I build them. That's how Gaia Sentinel started: a school project that turned into a real environmental monitoring platform with custom hardware. Now it's deployed and running.

I've also done 5+ years of art training at Global Art, which sounds unrelated but helps a lot with UI design. If something looks good and works well, that's not a coincidence.
